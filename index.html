About me

Hi, I study math-physics at Reed College, currently beginning my fourth year in this beautiful city called Portland in Oregon. Tracing back my roots, my memories of childhood and teenage years are sealed in Luzhou and Chengdu, China.

For the past three years, I've had the opportunity to work with the most wonderful people, conducting research in the fields of quantum chemistry, exotic compact objects, gravitational waves, and statistical inference. Moving on, I wish to continue studying the nature of gravity and those currently invisible objects with the advent of gravitational waves. The future is veild in mist, but I shall keep my curiosity and love for every bit of life, no matter which quantum state I'm gonna collapse into.
